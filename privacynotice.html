Data Protection

Global Privacy Notice


Introduction

This Privacy Notice applies to Ksoftcode, in the following referred to (collectively, “Ksoftcode LLC,” “we” and “us”). Ksoftcode respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with us, in compliance with relevant data protection laws.


1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Such as your name, email address, phone number, and other identifiable information when you interact with our services.

  • Technical Information: Including IP addresses, browser type, access times, and referring websites.

  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies to enhance the user experience on our website, track website performance, and store preferences. You can control cookies via your browser settings.


2. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage services you request.

  • To personalize user experience and communicate updates, promotions, and product offerings.

  • To improve our website, services, and internal processes through analysis of user behavior and technical data.

  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.


3. Sharing and Disclosure of Data

We may share your personal data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors assisting with operational services such as cloud storage, hosting, analytics, or customer support.

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process.

We ensure all partners adhere to strict privacy obligations to protect your data.


4. International Transfers

Your personal information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. Ksoftcode ensures such transfers are made in compliance with applicable laws and that adequate safeguards are in place.


5. Data Security

We employ appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. This includes encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.


6. Your Rights

Under data protection laws, you have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.

  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate data.

  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.

  • Restriction and Objection: Request limitations on how we use your data or object to specific uses.

To exercise these rights, have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy notice, please send a detailed message to privacy@ksoftcode.com or by mail to the address listed above.


7. Retention of Data

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.


8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Ksoftcode reserves the right to amend this Privacy Policy at any time. Any updates will be posted on our website, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically.


9. Changes to the Privacy Notice

We may amend this Privacy Notice to reflect changes in the law, our companies, our Services, our data collection use and practices, or advances in technology. Our use of the information we collect is subject to the Privacy Notice in effect at the time such information is used. Depending on the type of change, we may notify you of the change by posting on this page. Please carefully review any changes made to this Privacy Notice.
